Artist Spotlight

Jordan Rakei

A soulful instrumentalist blending R&B, jazz, and reggae

Known for his rich voice and introspective lyrics, Jordan Rakei has released multiple albums, with Wallflower and Origin recieving critical acclaim

MF DOOM

Rapper and producer known for his intricate rhymes and comic book persona

Influential in underground hip-hop, MF DOOM's albums, such as Madvillainy and MM..FOOD, are widely praised for their innovative lyricism and beats

Earl Sweatshirt

A groundbreaking voice in alternative hip-hop, known for his raw lyrics

With albums like Some Rap Songs and Doris, Earl has pushed the boundaries of hip-hop, exploring themes of self-reflection and mental health

Nai Palm

Lead singer of Hiatus Kaiyote, blending soul, jazz, and R&B with unique vocals

Nai Palm's work with Hiatus Kaiyote, especially in the album Choose Your Weapon, has been influential in the neo-soul and future soul genres
